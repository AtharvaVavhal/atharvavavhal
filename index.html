§ 01 — how it works
How it works

Three steps to zero queues.

No apps to download. No cash needed. Upload, pay, collect.

01
📄

Upload your document

Visit your campus portal on any device. Upload a PDF or doc, choose settings — pages, copies, B&W or colour, single or double sided. Takes under 30 seconds.

02
💳

Pay via Razorpay

One tap via UPI, card, or wallet. PCI-DSS compliant checkout. Confirms in under 10 seconds. No cash, no coins, no waiting at a counter to pay.

03
✅

Walk in and collect

Shop gets an instant WhatsApp + dashboard alert. Walk in, show your order ID, and you're done. Average wait time: under 2 minutes.
