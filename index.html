How it works

Three steps to zero queues.

No apps to download. No cash needed. Just upload, pay, and pick up your prints.

01
📄

Upload your document

Students visit their campus portal on any device, upload a PDF, and choose settings — pages, copies, B&W or colour, single or double sided.

02
💳

Pay via Razorpay

One-tap payment via UPI, cards, or wallets through Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant checkout. Confirm in under 10 seconds. No cash, no coins.

03
✅

Walk in and collect

Shop gets an instant WhatsApp + dashboard alert. You walk in, show your order ID, and leave. Average wait: under 2 minutes.
